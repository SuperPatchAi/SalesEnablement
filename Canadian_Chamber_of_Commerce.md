## 5. CANADIAN WORKPLACE WELLNESS MARKET

### 5.1 Workplace Health Challenges in Canada

**Sleep Disorders:**
- **Prevalence:** 30-40% of Canadian workers report sleep problems
- **Cost to economy:** $21.4 billion annually (absenteeism, reduced productivity)
- **Average lost work days:** 9.5 days/year for sleep-deprived employees
- **Pharmaceutical use:** 3.4 million Canadians use sleep medications

**Chronic Pain:**
- **Prevalence:** 20% of Canadian adults (6.4 million people)
- **Work-related:** 47% report chronic pain affects work performance
- **Cost to economy:** $38.3 billion annually (healthcare, lost productivity)
- **Pharmaceutical use:** 2.1 million use prescription pain medications regularly

**Mental Health and Stress:**
- **Prevalence:** 25% of Canadians experience high workplace stress
- **Mental health conditions:** 1 in 5 Canadians (20%) in any given year
- **Cost to economy:** $50 billion annually (absenteeism, presenteeism, turnover)
- **Sick leave:** Mental health is #1 cause of disability claims (30% of all claims)

**Physical Inactivity:**
- **Sedentary workers:** 62% of office workers lack regular physical activity
- **Obesity:** 30% of Canadian workers (up from 23% in 2010)
- **Musculoskeletal disorders:** Leading cause of work absence
