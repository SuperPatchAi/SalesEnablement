# Canadian Business Market Sales Enablement

## Overview

This folder contains sales enablement materials specifically designed for the **Canadian business market**, targeting 200,000+ businesses through the Chamber of Commerce network.

## Target Market

| Segment | Size | Key Contacts |
|---------|------|--------------|
| **Chamber Network** | 400+ chambers, 200,000+ businesses | Chamber executives, member services |
| **Chambers Plan** | 30,000 businesses, 300,000 employees | Benefits advisors, HR contacts |
| **Enterprise** | 100-1,000+ employees | HR Directors, Benefits Managers |
| **SMB** | 5-99 employees | Business Owners, CEOs |

## Market Opportunity

- **Total Market:** $600M-900M CAD annually
- **Corporate Wellness Market:** $17.4B CAD
- **Year 1 Target:** $56M-104M CAD
- **Projected ROI for Customers:** 300-500%+
